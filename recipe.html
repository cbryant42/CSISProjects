Chocolate Chip Cookies

I think everyone knows what chocolate chip cookies are, but in case you, don't they are a desert made of basic ingredients like chocolate chips, flour, sugar. Chocolate chip cookies are super simple to make and who doesn't love them?!




    Ingredients:
  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 1/2 tsp. salt



      • Steps:
    1. Mix together the sugar, brown sugar, butter, vanilla and egg in a bowl.
    2. Slowly add in flour, baking soda, and salt.
    3. Drop rounded spoonfuls of dough on cookie sheet 2 inches apart.
    4. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes.
    5. Cool on wire rack and enjoy!
